HELLO

Lauren is a New Mexico native who proudly has set up shop in the heart of Old Town Albuquerque. She is known for shaping gorgeous brows and paving the path for clients to find confidence in their own skin.

Lauren's battle with her own skin led her to find her passion of skincare and helping people feel beautiful. Specializing in corrective facial treatments such as chemical peels, microdermabrasion and mini facials she is known for her carefully curated skin care regimens utilizing the Rhonda Allison skincare line. Lauren's approach to skin has always been "less is more" and it is her goal to give each client healthy glowing skin.


Lauren graduated from the Aveda Insitute of New Mexico in 2016. She is trained in Aveda, PCA and Rhonda Allison. Currently using the Rhonda Allison line which blends ingredients derived from nature and science to deliver and transform the skin.
